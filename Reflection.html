Reflection

I believe that ocean clean-up is something that we need to think about when deciding what products we buy, something as simple as changing to multi-use plastics can have a huge change on the amount of pollution that goes into our oceans, we need to hold major polluting companies and countries accountable for their waste and begin to completely phase out plastics that can easily create garbage patches or lead to more waste in the ocean.

A project as simple as Team Seas has raised 20 Million dollars to remove 20 Million pounds of of trash from our oceans, community funded projects like these lead to change that affects all of us. https://www.teamseas.org/
