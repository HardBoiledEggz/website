A look into the largest contributors to waste in the Oceans.

Ocean Pollution Statistics

In a study by a team of researchers from the United States and Australia analyzed plastic waste sources and the levels in the world's ocean, mismanaged plastic waste from China was estimated to be about 8.80 million metric tons with 3.53 million metric tons ending up in the ocean.

Until recently China was taking in most of the worlds plastic waste but has now chosen to cut off imports of plastic waste, this is a contributing factor to why they were at the top of the list statistically. All major countries have contributed to plastic waste including the United States while they may not directly mismanage their waste they still produce some of the most plastic waste in the world.
