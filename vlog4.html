How can I reduce waste?

Steps to take to reduce your overall carbon footprint and waste into the ocean

Ways to reduce waste

Reducing the Use of Single-Use Plastics - Straws, Plastic Bags, Containers, Cups are some of the biggeste uses of single use plastics.
Recycling Properly - using plastics that can be recycled or cutting out plastics completely by using other products such as paper and cardboard which can be recycled can reduce your waste stream significantly.
Improving wasteward mangement can also reduce the amount of untreated water and waste from humans that end up as runoffs into the ocean. Getting envolved poltically - call your local state representitvies and encourage bills that help reduce waste streams and encourage carbon netural alternatives can lead to cleaner oceans and a cleaner earth.

Going Green - finding ways to reduce your waste stream entirely with Carbon Neutral products and Green alternatives can tone down the waste you put out expotentally, making it less likely the waste you do create ending up in the ocean.
